Welcome to the Pokemon Game


Gameplay

- Each pokemon has their respective health, abilities and Action Points (AP).

- Each attack uses various amounts of AP, and does various amounts of damage to the opponent.

- Each attack subtracts from your AP; your turn ends when there is not enough AP left to do any attack.

- User can earn money after each battle

- Money can be used to buy Pokemons or to upgrade them.

- Game is over when all pokemon available have been obtained and user has the option to play again
